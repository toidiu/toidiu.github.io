quiche is Cloudflare's implementation of the QUIC transport protocol and HTTP/3
as specified by the [IETF](https://quicwg.org/).

See the Cloudflare
[blog](https://blog.cloudflare.com/enjoy-a-slice-of-quic-and-rust) for more
details about the library.
