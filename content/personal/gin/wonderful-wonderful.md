- 2 Parts Gin
- ½ Lime juice
- ½ Lemon juice
- 1 Parts Simple Syrup
- 1 Egg white

First, vigorously shake egg white with ice to create foam. Add all ingredients with ice, shake and then strain into a chilled cocktail glass. Garnish with lemon twist.
