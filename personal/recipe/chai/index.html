Chai

2026-06-04

Ingredients

Spices

  • 2 cups Water
  • 2 Cloves
  • 2 Black peppercorns
  • 1 inch Ginger (grated or crushed)
  • Lemongrass (cut up)
  • 4 pods Cardamom (crushed)
  • 2 tbsp Black tea
  • 1/4 to 1/2 cup Milk
  • Sugar or palm sugar (to taste)

Optional

  • 1/2 Star anise (optional)
  • 1 piece Cinnamon (optional)

Preparation

Spices

  • Start to boil water on medium heat.
  • Add all spices.

Tea leaves

  • When water starts boiling, add black tea.
  • Let it simmer (not boil) for about 2 minutes — boiling extracts bad flavors.

Milk and sugar

  • Add sugar and milk to taste.
  • Turn heat to high and boil until it rises up.

Finish

  • Let it rise back down, then simmer on medium for 2 minutes.
  • Boil longer for a more concentrated flavor.
